## Abstract
Evaluates personalized tool-augmented LLMs from dual perspectives of personalization and proactivity. Proposes evaluation frameworks that assess how well LLMs can personalize tool usage and exhibit proactive behavior in assisting users, addressing the gap in existing benchmarks that focus on either personalization or proactivity separately.

## Key Contributions
- Proposes evaluation framework combining personalization and proactivity perspectives
- Addresses gap in existing benchmarks that treat personalization and proactivity separately
- Evaluates personalized tool-augmented LLMs on dual dimensions
- Published at ACL 2025
