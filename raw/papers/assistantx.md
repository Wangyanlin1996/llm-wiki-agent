## Abstract
Current service robots lack proactive collaboration awareness. We introduce AssistantX, an LLM-powered proactive assistant with a multi-agent framework (4 specialized LLM agents for perception, planning, decision-making, and reflective review). Built a dataset of 210 real-world tasks. Experiments over 1.5 months in a real office environment demonstrate AssistantX can reactively respond, actively adjust strategies, and proactively seek assistance from humans for task completion.

## Key Contributions
- Introduces AssistantX, an LLM-powered proactive assistant for human-populated environments
- Designs multi-agent framework with 4 specialized agents (perception, planning, decision-making, reflective review)
- Built dataset of 210 real-world tasks
- 1.5-month real office environment deployment experiment
- Demonstrates reactive response, active strategy adjustment, and proactive human assistance seeking
