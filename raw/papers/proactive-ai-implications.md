## Abstract
Investigates the implications of proactive AI-based agents on perceived competence and system satisfaction in human-AI collaboration contexts. Examines how proactive behavior by AI agents affects user perceptions and collaboration outcomes, providing empirical evidence on the trade-offs between proactivity and user experience.

## Key Contributions
- Investigates implications of proactive AI agents on competence perception and satisfaction
