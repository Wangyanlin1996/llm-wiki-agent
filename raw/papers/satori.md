## Abstract
Augmented Reality assistance is increasingly used for physical tasks like assembly and cooking. Most systems rely on reactive responses. We present Satori, a novel AR system that proactively guides users by modeling both mental states and environmental contexts. Satori integrates the Belief-Desire-Intention (BDI) framework with multi-modal LLM to deliver contextually appropriate guidance. Evaluated with sixteen within-subject study, Satori matches the performance of designer-created Wizard-of-Oz systems, without manual configurations, improving generalizability and reusability.

## Key Contributions
- Introduces Satori, a proactive AR assistant with BDI user modeling
- Integrates Belief-Desire-Intention framework with multi-modal LLM
- Proactively guides users by modeling mental states and environmental contexts
- Matches Wizard-of-Oz performance without manual configurations
- Improves generalizability and reusability over reactive AR systems
