## Summary

5G Network Management encompasses the set of 3GPP specifications for managing and orchestrating 5G mobile networks, defined primarily in the TS 28.x series. It covers management services (MnS) for provisioning, fault supervision, performance management, and policy management, among others.

## Key Specification Family

- TS 28.532 — Generic management services
- TS 28.555 — Policy management Stage 1 (requirements)
- TS 28.556 — Policy management Stage 2 & 3 (information model + solution sets)
- TS 28.622 — Generic NRM IRP Information Service
