## Summary

3GPP TS 28.532 defines generic management services (provisioning MnS) including operations (createMOI, deleteMOI, modifyMOIAttributes, getMOIAttributes) and notifications (notifyMOICreation, notifyMOIDeletion, notifyMOIAttributeValueChange, notifyMOIChanges) that serve as the foundation for specific management services like [[PolicyMnS]].

## Relevance to Policy MnS

Policy MnS reuses all generic provisioning MnS operations and notifications from TS 28.532, treating policies as managed object instances. The RESTful HTTP and YANG/NETCONF solution sets for Policy MnS also leverage the generic provisioning MnS resource and data type definitions.
