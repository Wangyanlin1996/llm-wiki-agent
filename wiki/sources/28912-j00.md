## Summary

3GPP TR 28.912 is the Rel-18 study report that investigated key issues for intent-driven management and proposed solutions that were subsequently incorporated into the normative TS 28.312. It studied RAN energy saving via intent, intent conflict classification (target/expectation/intent), 5GC intent expectations, IntentReport IOC design, closed-loop composition, feasibility check, intent handling capability exposure, AI/ML entity capability mapping, SON orchestration driven by intent, MDA function integration, and collaboration with TM Forum on intent interface deployment scenarios.

## Key Claims

- Intent-driven RAN energy saving should introduce new expectation targets for RAN Energy Consumption and RAN Energy Efficiency, enabling operators to balance energy saving with service experience targets
- Three intent conflict scenarios identified: target conflict (within same expectation), expectation conflict (within same intent), intent conflict (between different intents); MnS consumer can provide conflict handling guidelines as contexts
- IntentReport IOC proposed with three report types: intent fulfilment information, intent conflict information, and intent feasibility check information
- IntentHandlingFunction IOC proposed with supportedIntentHandlingCapability dataType to expose expectation objects and targets supported by each handling function
- IntentAdminState attribute (ACTIVATED/DEACTIVATED) proposed for intent suspension/resume mechanism
- Observation time attribute proposed for evaluating fulfilment information over defined periods
- AI/ML capability mapping proposed via AIML_capability_mapping_report dataType, enabling consumers to discover and request specific AI/ML capabilities for intent fulfilment
- Intent-driven SON orchestration classifies intents into three types (control parameter-only, KPI-only, mixed) and detects contradictions within SON execution
- Intent-driven MDA integration: MnS producer checks whether ExpectationTargets are within supportedMDACapabilities of correlated MDA functions before requesting analysis
- TM Forum collaboration: two deployment scenarios proposed — scenario#1 uses 3GPP IDMS for all interfaces; scenario#2 uses TMF intent API for CSC-CSP and 3GPP IDMS for CSP-NOP/NOP-NEP
